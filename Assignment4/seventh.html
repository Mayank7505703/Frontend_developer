The Art of Digital Typography

An exploration of fonts, spacing, and style

Typography is more than just picking fonts; it's about arranging text in a readable and aesthetically pleasing way. Good typography guides the reader's eye.

We can use different properties to control the look. This paragraph has increased letter-spacing and line-height. We can also highlight certain words, or add emphasis with italic text. Notice how the font-family and font-size create a clear hierarchy between the heading and the body text.
